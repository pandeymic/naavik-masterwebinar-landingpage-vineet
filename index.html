About the Initiative

The Naavik Master Webinar Series is designed to bridge the gap between aspiring maritime professionals and the industry's top experts. Our mission is to provide clear, actionable guidance to students and young professionals, demystifying careers at sea and on shore. We connect you directly with Captains, Chief Engineers, and thought leaders to shape the next generation of the maritime workforce.

Key Highlights

Ask Anything
